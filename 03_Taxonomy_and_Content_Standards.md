# Content Standards

Every book entry must contain:

1. Book Analysis
2. Leadership Transition Commentary
3. Practical Applications
4. Reflection Questions
5. Cross-Book Wisdom Connections
6. Related Books

---

# Quality Standard

Readers should be able to answer three questions after every article:

1. What did the author teach?
2. Why does it matter for leadership?
3. How does it apply to major life transitions?

If an entry does not answer all three questions, it is incomplete.
